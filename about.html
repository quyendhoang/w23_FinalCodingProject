About

This website was created by Quyen Hoang, a first year Master's student at the University of Michigan School of Information for SI 539: Web Design in Winter 2023. Quyen completed her undergraduate degree at Michigan State University and graduate school has been taxing journey. A way Quyen has managed her work-school-life balance is through watching her alma mater's basketball team.

To practice her coding skills, Quyen has created this website as an overview of the 2022-23 Mens Basketball Team and their season.

References: Images and data retrieved from Michigan State Spartan Athletics website.
